RULES :


After selecting the level, each player must, in a limited number of moves and in total darkness, find the exit of the temple. To help him in his quest, the player will be able to collect torches that will increase his visibility area, but he will also be hindered by doors that can only be opened by levers, as well as holes that will make him start again at the beginning of the room.

In the creation mode, you will be able to create your own level and thus face them.

Good luck, you will need it!
